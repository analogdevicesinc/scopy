Data Logger Channel Customization

The Data Logger now includes channel customization capabilities that let you personalize how channels are displayed throughout the interface. You can customize channel display names and override unit of measurement information to better match your specific application, test setup, or documentation requirements. These customizations apply across all display modes including plots, text displays, and seven segment displays.

Key Features

  • Custom Display Names: Rename channels to meaningful names that reflect your specific application or test setup
  • Unit of Measurement Override: Customize both the unit name and symbol for any channel

How to Use

  1. Open the Data Logger tool and connect to your device.
  2. Click on any channel in the left menu to open the Channel Attributes panel on the right.
  3. To change the display name: Click on the channel name in the header of the attributes panel and edit it directly.
  4. To customize units: Find the "Unit Of Measurement" section and update the Name field (full unit name) and/or Symbol field (unit symbol).
  5. Your customizations will immediately apply to all displays and be preserved in your session.
