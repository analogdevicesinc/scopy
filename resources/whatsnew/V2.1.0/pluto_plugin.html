AD936x Plugin

Scopy now offers dedicated support for AD936x devices with Scopy.

The new AD936x Tool enables you to integrate and control AD936x-based devices (including PlutoSDR, AD-FMCOMMS2/3/4/5) within Scopy. It provides features such as device discovery and device configurationâ€”including center frequency, bandwidth, sample rate, and gain.

For advanced users, the AD936x Advanced Tool offers access to low-level settings for AD936x devices. This tool allows you to test and customize device driver initialization options and values, giving you deeper control over hardware and driver properties.

How to Use

  1. Connect your AD936x device to your computer.
  2. Open Scopy and select the AD936x or AD936x Advanced from the available instruments.
