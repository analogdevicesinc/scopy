AD9084 (Apollo) Plugin

Scopy now offers dedicated support for the AD9084 (Apollo MXFE QUAD) devices. You can upload and manage custom FIR filters for Apollo devices directly from the Scopy interface.

The new AD9084 (Apollo) Tool allows you to integrate and control AD9084-based devices within Scopy.

How to Use

  1. Connect your AD9084 (Apollo) device to your computer.
  2. Open Scopy and select the Apollo tool from the instrument list.
